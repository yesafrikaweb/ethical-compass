Module 02: The Workbench

Resilience Testing

Stress-testing institutional integrity before failure occurs. The Workbench environment simulates high-impact integrity challenges and complex risk scenarios to measure resilience.

The "Strategic Stress" Governance Model

Most governance systems are built for compliant operations. Ethical Compass is built for reality. Our Resilience Testing framework proactively probes your institution's protocols for gaps that complex risk scenarios might exploit.

  • Simulation Framework Adaptive Resilience (ART) Framework
  • Attack Library 500+ pre-configured reputational and integrity vectors
  • Resilience Metric Dynamic Integrity Response Score (DIRS)
Step 01: Vector Generation

Scenario Synthesis

Synthesizing credible risk scenarios based on current industry trends and emerging vulnerabilities.

Step 02: Execution

Stress-Test Pulse

Applying simulated pressure to internal protocols to detect cracks in oversight.

Step 03: Validation

Remediation Mapping

Mapping failure points directly to actionable governance patches.

Simulated Environments

Specific analytical focus areas within the Workbench module.

Reputational Crisis Sandbox

Simulate a high-impact media or social leakage event to test internal communication silos and response precision.

Supply Chain Ethics Probe

Stress-test third-party due diligence layers by simulating a discovery of ethical violations in Tier-3 suppliers.

Internal Protocol Safeguards

Simulating unauthorized executive actions to verify that institutional guardrails cannot be bypassed by high-level credentials.

Regulatory Stress-Testing

Projecting the impact of sudden, aggressive regulatory changes on existing project viability.
